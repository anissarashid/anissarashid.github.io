Redirect

Timeline: 5 weeks
Team: 5 people

Redirect played with angles and vantage points to redirect the viewers perception of the images through these interesting shot types. Our team told the story of a couple as a unit and then as an individual, and follows their progression by capturing the feelings of empowerment and grief.

Role: First shoot working as a Creative Director in training. Worked with Indigo Vintage and directed styling, drew out a comprehensive shot list, scouted several potential locations, and helped with photo selection and post-processing of the images.
Photographer: Kendall Halliburton
