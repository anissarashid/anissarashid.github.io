Matriarchs

Timeline: 5 weeks
Team: 5 people

The theme for Matriarchs was centered around the celebration of age and beauty. Inspired by the eccentric fashion icon Iris Apfel, we styled our models in vibrant and powerful clothing. The models wore designer garments pulled from M.A.C, a high-end retailer in San Francisco.

Role: Pulled clothing and directed styling, scouted location (Bancroft Hotel), contacted models, assisted with makeup, and directed shots day of.
Photographer: Lieyah Dagan
